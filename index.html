About me

Portuguese, based in Lisbon. I've spent the last 4+ years at early-stage startups, mostly in BD and partnerships, more recently building products with AI tooling. I tend to show up early on a project and figure out how things should actually work, whether that's a partner pipeline, an internal tool, or a full product.

I'm proactive and self-sufficient. Give me a problem and I'll figure out the systems, the product, and the story without waiting for someone to hand me a playbook. I do well working solo, and just as well across teams.

How I work

The systems come before the relationships

Most of my BD work starts with setting up what doesn't exist yet: the CRM, the points program, the Discord, the partner pack, the deck, sometimes a small app to run all of it. Once that's in place, the relationships and the deals come a lot easier.

I translate complex into simple

Most of the projects I've worked on had deeply technical products. MPC custody, Bitcoin interoperability, satellite infrastructure. My job was turning that into something a partner, LP, or user could actually understand and act on.

AI is part of how I work, not a talking point

I vibecode internal tools, build AI workflows for research and outreach, and automate ops with whatever's available. If a tool I need doesn't exist yet, I'll usually just build it.
