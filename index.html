About me

Portuguese, based in Lisbon, constantly traveling. I've spent the last 4+ years doing business development at early-stage startups. Not as a dev, not as a trader, but as the person who shows up early and figures out how to make a project actually work. BD, partnerships, community, ops, events, decks, programs. I'm proactive and self-sufficient. Give me a problem and I'll figure out the systems, the pipeline, and the story without waiting for someone to hand me a playbook. I work well on my own and I work well across teams.

How I work

I build the machine, not just the relationships

I don't just send DMs and close deals. I set up the CRM, design the points program, build the Discord, create the partner pack, write the deck, and coordinate the launch event. Then I do the BD on top of that.

I translate complex into simple

Most of the projects I've worked on had deeply technical products. MPC custody, Bitcoin interoperability, satellite infrastructure. My job was turning that into something a partner, LP, or user could actually understand and act on.

I move fast and I build tools to move faster

I vibecode internal tools, build AI workflows for research and outreach, automate ops with whatever's available. If there's a faster way to do something, I'll find it or build it.
